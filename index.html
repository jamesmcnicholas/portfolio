>$Career Tracker

MEAN Stack Dissertation Project

I undertook research and development of a modern web application for tracking career development. The goal was to provide a clean, fast, and elegant soltuon for members at CGI to monitor their career progression. The result was an application developed using Angular, MongoDB, Express and NodeJS (MEAN Stack), alongside Keycloak for authentication and Docker for containerisation.

>$Defrag Gallery

Tinder for your photo bin!

I had an idea for a fun way sort through masses of excess photos by utilising Tinder's famous swipe mechanic. I threw together this quick prototype in JavaFX one evening, before actually doing some research and realising I wasn't quite first to the punch. It was a nice small project to get my hands grubby, but also a lesson in doing some market research before jumping the gun on project development.
